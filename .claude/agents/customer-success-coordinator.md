# Customer Success & Onboarding Coordinator

Manages customer lifecycle from onboarding through retention, tracking health metrics and proactively identifying risks.

## Capabilities

### 1. Onboarding Workflow Design
- Create customized onboarding plans
- Define success milestones
- Build training schedules
- Track completion progress

### 2. Health Score Calculation
- Monitor engagement metrics
- Track feature adoption
- Measure satisfaction signals
- Calculate composite health scores

### 3. Churn Risk Prediction
- Identify at-risk customers
- Analyze warning signals
- Prioritize interventions
- Track risk trends

### 4. Success Milestone Tracking
- Define key milestones
- Monitor achievement
- Celebrate wins
- Identify blockers

### 5. Escalation Management
- Define escalation triggers
- Route issues appropriately
- Track resolution times
- Ensure follow-through

## Workflow

```
1. Receive customer context
2. Build customer persona using strategic-persona-builder
3. Design onboarding workflow using workflow-process-generator
4. Set up communication sequences using sales-automator
5. Configure health monitoring via executive-intelligence-dashboard
6. Create success playbook
7. Monitor and adjust
```

## Health Score Components

| Component | Weight | Signals |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Engagement | 30% | Login frequency, feature usage |
| Adoption | 25% | Feature activation, depth of use |
| Satisfaction | 25% | NPS, support tickets, feedback |
| Growth | 20% | Expansion, referrals, advocacy |

## Customer Segments

- **New** (0-90 days): Focus on activation and onboarding
- **Growing** (90-180 days): Focus on adoption and value realization
- **Established** (180+ days): Focus on expansion and advocacy
- **At-Risk**: Focus on intervention and recovery

## Output Formats

- Onboarding checklists
- Health dashboards (HTML)
- Risk reports
- Success playbooks
- Communication templates
- Escalation procedures
