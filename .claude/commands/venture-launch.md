# Venture Launch Package

**Workflow Template**: Complete launch materials for a new client venture

---

## Workflow Steps

Execute the following skills in sequence for venture go-to-market:

### Step 1: Market Research & Validation
**Skill**: `open-deep-research-team`

Conduct comprehensive market analysis:
- Market size and dynamics
- Competitive landscape
- Target customer segments
- Regulatory considerations
- Go-to-market opportunities

### Step 2: Build Target Personas
**Skill**: `strategic-persona-builder`

Create customer and stakeholder personas:
- Primary customer persona(s)
- Partner/channel personas
- Investor persona (if fundraising)
- Vianeo-format for platform entry
- Jobs-to-be-Done analysis

### Step 3: Create Portfolio Page
**Skill**: `360-client-portfolio-builder`

Build professional venture showcase:
- Production-quality HTML portfolio
- Value proposition messaging
- Traction and validation highlights
- Team and partnership credibility
- Clear investor/partner CTAs

### Step 4: Develop Outreach Campaign
**Skill**: `sales-automator`

Create go-to-market outreach:
- Investor outreach sequence
- Partner introduction emails
- Customer acquisition messaging
- Press/media pitch templates

### Step 5: Design Polish
**Skill**: `design-director`

Elevate all materials to professional quality:
- Portfolio page visual refinement
- Pitch deck polish (if applicable)
- Consistent brand application
- Accessibility compliance

---

## Required Input

Provide:
1. **Venture Name & Description**
2. **Vianeo Sprint Data** (if available)
3. **Target Market** - Geography, segments
4. **Stage** - Pre-seed, seed, Series A, etc.
5. **Launch Goals** - Fundraising, partnerships, customers
6. **Brand Assets** - Logo, colors, images

---

## Output Deliverables

1. **Market Research Brief** (Markdown)
   - Comprehensive market analysis
   - Competitive positioning map
   - Opportunity assessment

2. **Persona Package** (Markdown + JSON)
   - 3-5 key personas
   - Vianeo platform-ready versions
   - Strategic recommendations

3. **Portfolio Website** (HTML)
   - Single-page production site
   - Mobile-responsive design
   - Deployment-ready

4. **Outreach Package** (Email templates)
   - Investor sequence (5-7 emails)
   - Partner introduction templates
   - Customer acquisition sequence

5. **Launch Checklist** (Markdown)
   - Pre-launch tasks
   - Launch day activities
   - Post-launch follow-up

---

## Execution Instructions

1. Gather venture context and Vianeo data
2. Run market research (30-45 min)
3. Build personas from research (20-25 min)
4. Create portfolio page (2-3 hours)
5. Develop outreach sequences (20-30 min)
6. Apply design polish (20-30 min)
7. Final review and deployment prep

---

## Timeline Options

**Sprint Mode** (1 day):
- Essential research + portfolio + basic outreach

**Standard Mode** (2-3 days):
- Full research + personas + portfolio + outreach

**Comprehensive Mode** (1 week):
- Everything + investor deck + press kit + launch event materials
