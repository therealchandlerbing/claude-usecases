# Morning Executive Routine

**Workflow Template**: Multi-skill morning startup sequence

---

## Workflow Steps

Execute the following skills in sequence:

### Step 1: Daily CEO Intelligence Brief
**Skill**: `ceo-advisor`

Generate the morning executive brief covering:
- Critical issues requiring immediate attention
- Intelligence scan across internal and external sources
- Stakeholder updates and relationship health
- Today's priorities and focus areas

### Step 2: Scan Recent Communications
**Skill**: `intelligence-extractor`

Process overnight communications for actionable intelligence:
- Scan recent emails for partnership/funding signals
- Extract stakeholder mentions and commitments
- Identify items requiring follow-up
- Flag urgent matters not in the brief

### Step 3: Quick Project Status Check
**Skill**: `360-executive-project-tracker` (optional, if time permits)

Review project dashboard for:
- Any new blockers flagged overnight
- Tasks due today
- Team capacity alerts

---

## Output Deliverables

1. **Morning Brief Dashboard** (HTML) - Comprehensive intelligence overview
2. **Intelligence Extract** (JSON/summary) - New signals from communications
3. **Priority Action List** - Top 3-5 items for the day

---

## Execution Instructions

When this workflow is invoked:

1. Start with the CEO brief generation (15-20 min)
2. While brief generates, note any specific communications to scan
3. Run intelligence extraction on recent emails/messages (5-10 min)
4. Synthesize findings into a unified morning briefing
5. Highlight any conflicts or additions from the intelligence scan

---

## Customization Options

**Quick Mode** (10 min):
- CEO brief only, skip detailed extraction

**Standard Mode** (20-25 min):
- Full brief + communications scan

**Deep Mode** (35-40 min):
- Full brief + extraction + project tracker review

---

## Trigger Phrases

- "Run my morning routine"
- "Morning startup"
- "Start my day"
- "What do I need to know this morning?"
