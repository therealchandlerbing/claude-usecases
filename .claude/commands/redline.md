# Review and Redline Contract

Perform attorney-quality contract review using the **Contract Redlining Tool** skill.

## Instructions

Invoke the `contract-redlining-tool` skill to analyze contracts against 360's standard positions and generate professional redlines.

## Required Context

Before review, provide:
1. **Deal Type** - Client services, partnership, MoU, JV, university, other
2. **Strategic Importance** - Critical / High / Medium / Standard
3. **Power Dynamics** - We need them / Balanced / They need us
4. **Geographic Context** - Domestic / Brazil / Latin America / Europe
5. **Timeline** - Rush / Normal / Patient
6. **Key Concerns** - Payment, IP, liability, scope, termination, etc.

## What Gets Generated

**Risk Analysis:**
- Critical issues (deal-breakers)
- High-priority concerns
- Standard improvements

**Redlined Document:**
- Tracked changes with protective language
- Margin comments explaining business impact
- Negotiation approach for each issue
- Compromise options

**Executive Summary:**
- Overall risk assessment
- Top 3-5 issues requiring attention
- Recommended negotiation strategy

## Risk Classification

- **CRITICAL**: Unlimited liability, IP assignment, payment contingent on subjective satisfaction
- **HIGH**: Excessive indemnification, scope creep risk, weak termination rights
- **STANDARD**: Missing audit rights, vague deliverables, non-optimal payment terms

## Cultural Intelligence

Applies cultural negotiation considerations for international deals, particularly Brazilian partnerships with appropriate governing law and arbitration provisions.
