# Partnership Evaluation

**Workflow Template**: Research → Intelligence → Proposal for partnership opportunities

---

## Workflow Steps

Execute the following skills in sequence when evaluating a partnership:

### Step 1: Partner Research
**Skill**: `open-deep-research-team`

Conduct comprehensive due diligence:
- Organization background and mission
- Leadership and key stakeholders
- Financial health indicators
- Strategic priorities and initiatives
- Reputation and track record
- Cultural considerations
- Potential synergies and conflicts

### Step 2: Extract Existing Intelligence
**Skill**: `intelligence-extractor`

Surface internal knowledge about this partner:
- Past conversations and interactions
- Previous proposals or agreements
- Relationship history
- Known contacts and champions
- Past concerns or issues

### Step 3: Stakeholder Mapping
**Skill**: `strategic-persona-builder`

Build personas for key partnership stakeholders:
- Primary decision-maker
- Internal champion
- Technical/operational lead
- Potential blockers
- Communication preferences

### Step 4: Partnership Proposal
**Skill**: `360-proposal-builder`

Draft partnership proposal:
- Value proposition (mutual benefit)
- Proposed structure
- Resource commitments
- Timeline and milestones
- Success metrics
- Risk allocation

### Step 5: Outreach Strategy
**Skill**: `sales-automator`

Create engagement plan:
- Initial outreach messaging
- Follow-up sequence
- Meeting agenda suggestions
- Objection handling

---

## Required Input

Provide:
1. **Partner Organization Name**
2. **Partnership Type** - Distribution, JV, strategic alliance, etc.
3. **Our Objective** - What do we want from this partnership?
4. **Known Contacts** - Any existing relationships?
5. **Timeline** - Urgency of partnership development
6. **Context** - How did this opportunity arise?

---

## Output Deliverables

1. **Due Diligence Report** (Markdown)
   - Comprehensive partner profile
   - Opportunity and risk assessment
   - Go/No-Go recommendation

2. **Internal Intelligence Summary** (Markdown)
   - Relationship history
   - Past interactions and outcomes
   - Known preferences and concerns

3. **Stakeholder Personas** (Markdown)
   - 2-3 key decision-maker personas
   - Communication recommendations
   - Influence mapping

4. **Partnership Proposal** (DOCX)
   - Mutual value proposition
   - Proposed terms and structure
   - Success metrics

5. **Engagement Plan** (Email templates + Timeline)
   - Outreach sequence
   - Meeting preparation
   - Follow-up cadence

---

## Execution Instructions

1. Gather initial context from user
2. Run partner research (25-35 min)
3. Extract internal intelligence (10-15 min)
4. Build stakeholder personas (15-20 min)
5. Draft partnership proposal (20-25 min)
6. Create outreach strategy (10-15 min)
7. Review complete package with user

---

## Partnership Assessment Matrix

| Factor | Weight | Assessment |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Strategic Fit | 25% | High/Medium/Low |
| Resource Requirements | 20% | Sustainable/Stretch/Prohibitive |
| Risk Level | 20% | Low/Medium/High |
| Relationship Quality | 15% | Strong/Developing/New |
| Market Opportunity | 20% | Significant/Moderate/Limited |
