# Generate Client Proposal

Create an executive-grade proposal using the **360 Proposal Builder** skill.

## Instructions

Invoke the `360-proposal-builder` skill to generate a customized proposal for the specified client/opportunity.

## Required Information

To generate the proposal, provide:
1. **Client/Organization Name**
2. **Service Track** (one of):
   - Innovation Assessment
   - Business Design
   - Leadership & Culture
3. **Discovery Context** - Key challenges and needs identified
4. **Cultural Context** - Region/communication style preferences

## Proposal Components

The generated proposal will include:

1. **Executive Summary** - Strategic positioning and value proposition
2. **Client Challenges** - Articulated understanding of their situation
3. **Proposed Solution** - Service package with specific deliverables
4. **Methodology** - GenIP-attributed approach and process
5. **Team & Expertise** - Relevant experience and case studies
6. **Investment** - Pricing and payment terms
7. **Next Steps** - Clear path forward

## Critical Requirements

- **GenIP Attribution**: Methodology properly credited to GenIP
- **Institutional Trust**: References GenIP's track record (MIT, Oxford, etc.)
- **Strategic Partner Positioning**: 360 as partner, not vendor
- **Cultural Adaptation**: Tone adjusted for client region

## Output Format

Generate as:
- DOCX document (primary)
- Executive summary for email/presentation

---

**Skill**: 360-proposal-builder
**Trigger**: "Create proposal for [client]" / "Generate proposal" / "Write proposal"
