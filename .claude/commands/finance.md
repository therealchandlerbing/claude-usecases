# Financial Modeling & Analysis

Access the comprehensive financial analysis suite using the **Financial Modeling Skills** skill.

## Instructions

Invoke the `financial-modeling-skills` skill for investment analysis, portfolio intelligence, and impact measurement.

## Sub-Skills Available

### 1. Investment Analysis
**Use for:** Evaluating equity investments, growth capital, acquisitions

**Capabilities:**
- Multi-scenario financial modeling (base/upside/downside)
- Returns analysis (IRR, MOIC, DPI, TVPI)
- Comparable company benchmarking
- Risk assessment and sensitivity analysis
- Exit strategy evaluation
- Investment committee documentation

**Output:** Excel model, IC memo, executive summary
**Time:** 50-70 minutes

---

### 2. Portfolio Intelligence
**Use for:** Portfolio performance, LP reports, allocation optimization

**Capabilities:**
- Cross-portfolio performance analytics
- Strategic allocation optimization
- Risk correlation analysis
- Impact measurement aggregation
- Quarterly reporting automation
- Interactive dashboard generation

**Output:** Portfolio dashboard (Excel/HTML), quarterly reports
**Time:** 40-60 minutes

---

### 3. Impact Modeling
**Use for:** SROI calculation, blended finance, SDG measurement

**Capabilities:**
- Social Return on Investment (SROI) calculation
- Blended finance structuring
- Impact-weighted accounting
- SDG contribution mapping
- Theory of change financial modeling
- Impact attribution analysis

**Output:** SROI report, impact dashboard, blended finance model
**Time:** 60-90 minutes

## Output Standards

All outputs are institutional-grade:
- Excel models with working formulas
- Investment committee memos
- Portfolio dashboards (HTML/Excel)
- Valuation reports with sensitivity tables
