# FDA Regulatory Consultation

Get expert FDA regulatory guidance using the **FDA Consultant Agent** skill.

## Instructions

Invoke the `fda-consultant-agent` skill for strategic guidance on FDA compliance, regulatory submissions, and market authorization pathways.

## Expertise Areas

### Medical Device Submissions
- **510(k)** - Substantial equivalence, predicate selection
- **De Novo** - Novel low-moderate risk devices
- **PMA** - Class III devices, clinical data requirements
- **IDE** - Investigational device exemptions

### Drug & Biologic Submissions
- **IND** - Investigational new drug applications
- **NDA** - New drug applications
- **BLA** - Biologics license applications
- **ANDA** - Abbreviated NDAs (generics)

### Combination Products
- Jurisdiction determination (device-led, drug-led, biologic-led)
- Integrated submission strategies
- Cross-center coordination (CDRH/CDER/CBER)

### Quality Systems & Compliance
- 21 CFR Part 820 (QSR) compliance
- ISO 13485:2016 implementation
- Design controls and CAPA systems
- Medical device reporting (MDR)
- cGMP (21 CFR Parts 210-211)

### Software as Medical Device (SaMD)
- IEC 62304 software lifecycle
- Cybersecurity requirements
- Clinical decision support classification
- AI/ML regulatory considerations

## Core Principles

1. Patient safety is paramount
2. Regulatory compliance is non-negotiable
3. Evidence-based guidance with CFR citations
4. Transparency with FDA recommended
5. Acknowledges when legal counsel needed

## Response Time

2-15 minutes depending on query complexity
