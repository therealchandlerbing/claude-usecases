# Deal Review & Risk Assessment

**Workflow Template**: Contract analysis + financial evaluation for deal decisions

---

## Workflow Steps

Execute the following skills in sequence when evaluating a deal:

### Step 1: Contract Risk Analysis
**Skill**: `contract-redlining-tool`

Review the contract for risks and issues:
- Critical deal-breakers identified
- High-priority concerns flagged
- Standard improvements recommended
- Negotiation strategy developed
- Redlined document produced

### Step 2: Financial Analysis
**Skill**: `financial-modeling-skills`

Evaluate the financial implications:
- Deal economics modeling
- Revenue/cost projections
- ROI/IRR analysis
- Sensitivity scenarios (base/upside/downside)
- Cash flow implications

### Step 3: Strategic Assessment
**Skill**: `ceo-advisor`

Assess strategic fit and implications:
- Alignment with company strategy
- Resource requirements
- Opportunity cost analysis
- Competitive implications
- Relationship value beyond financial

### Step 4: Risk Summary & Recommendation
**Skill**: `skill-orchestrator`

Synthesize findings into decision framework:
- Go/No-Go recommendation
- Key negotiation priorities
- Risk mitigation requirements
- Decision timeline

---

## Required Input

Provide:
1. **Contract Document** - PDF or text of the agreement
2. **Deal Context**:
   - Deal type (client, partnership, JV, etc.)
   - Strategic importance (Critical/High/Medium/Standard)
   - Power dynamics (We need them/Balanced/They need us)
   - Timeline (Rush/Normal/Patient)
3. **Financial Parameters**:
   - Expected revenue/value
   - Investment required
   - Timeline/duration
4. **Key Concerns** - Specific areas to focus on

---

## Output Deliverables

1. **Contract Analysis** (DOCX + Summary)
   - Redlined contract with tracked changes
   - Risk classification (Critical/High/Standard)
   - Margin comments with business context
   - Negotiation talking points

2. **Financial Model** (Excel)
   - Deal economics with scenarios
   - Sensitivity analysis
   - Key metrics (IRR, payback, etc.)

3. **Strategic Assessment** (Markdown)
   - Fit analysis
   - Resource implications
   - Competitive considerations

4. **Executive Decision Brief** (1-page)
   - Go/No-Go recommendation
   - Top 3 risks and mitigations
   - Negotiation priorities
   - Recommended next steps

---

## Execution Instructions

1. Receive contract and context from user
2. Run contract analysis (15-20 min)
3. Perform financial modeling (20-30 min)
4. Conduct strategic assessment (10-15 min)
5. Synthesize into decision brief (10 min)
6. Review with user and refine

---

## Decision Framework

| Signal | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| No critical risks + positive financials + strategic fit | **Proceed** |
| Manageable risks + neutral financials + strategic value | **Negotiate** |
| Critical risks OR negative financials | **Decline or major renegotiation** |
| Mixed signals | **Deeper diligence required** |

---
