# New Client Onboarding

**Workflow Template**: Research → Persona → Proposal sequence for new clients

---

## Workflow Steps

Execute the following skills in sequence when engaging a new client:

### Step 1: Deep Client Research
**Skill**: `open-deep-research-team`

Conduct comprehensive research on the prospective client:
- Organization background and history
- Leadership team and decision-makers
- Recent news, funding, strategic initiatives
- Industry position and competitive landscape
- Potential challenges and pain points
- Cultural context (especially for international clients)

### Step 2: Build Stakeholder Personas
**Skill**: `strategic-persona-builder`

Create evidence-backed personas for key stakeholders:
- Primary decision-maker persona
- Champion/internal advocate persona
- Technical evaluator persona (if applicable)
- Jobs-to-be-Done analysis
- Communication style preferences
- Decision-making patterns

### Step 3: Draft Initial Proposal
**Skill**: `360-proposal-builder`

Generate customized proposal based on research:
- Tailored value proposition
- Service track recommendation
- Relevant case studies and social proof
- Culturally-appropriate positioning
- Pricing options
- Clear next steps

### Step 4: Prepare Outreach Sequence
**Skill**: `sales-automator`

Create personalized engagement plan:
- Initial outreach email (warm introduction)
- Follow-up sequence (3-5 touches)
- Meeting preparation materials
- Objection handling notes

---

## Required Input

Provide:
1. **Client/Organization Name**
2. **Primary Contact** (if known)
3. **Opportunity Context** - How did they come to us?
4. **Initial Conversation Notes** (if any)
5. **Service Interest** - What are they looking for?

---

## Output Deliverables

1. **Client Research Brief** (Markdown)
   - Comprehensive background
   - Key insights and talking points
   - Risk/opportunity assessment

2. **Stakeholder Personas** (Markdown + JSON)
   - 2-3 key personas
   - Communication recommendations
   - Decision-making map

3. **Draft Proposal** (DOCX)
   - Ready for review and customization
   - GenIP attribution included
   - Cultural considerations applied

4. **Outreach Sequence** (Email templates)
   - Personalized messaging
   - Strategic timing recommendations

---

## Execution Instructions

1. Gather initial context from user
2. Run deep research (20-30 min)
3. Review research findings with user
4. Build personas based on research (15-20 min)
5. Generate proposal draft (15-20 min)
6. Create outreach sequence (10-15 min)
7. Review complete package

---

## Customization Options

**Quick Mode** (30 min):
- Express research + proposal draft only

**Standard Mode** (60-75 min):
- Full research + personas + proposal

**Comprehensive Mode** (90-120 min):
- Everything + outreach + meeting prep materials

---

## Trigger Phrases

- "New client onboarding for [company]"
- "Research and proposal for [prospect]"
- "Prepare for [company] engagement"
- "Client prep workflow"
