# Weekly Intelligence Brief

Generate the weekly 360 Impact Brief using the **Executive Intelligence Dashboard** skill.

## Instructions

Invoke the `executive-intelligence-dashboard` skill to create a production-quality weekly intelligence brief.

## Data Collection

Pull from multiple sources:

**Asana Workspace**
- Active project status and progress
- Tasks completed this week
- Critical upcoming deadlines
- Blockers and risks flagged
- Team capacity and assignments

**Gmail**
- Important stakeholder communications
- Partnership updates
- Client interactions
- Decision threads

**Google Drive**
- Recent document activity
- Strategic documents and proposals
- Meeting notes and action items

## Output Structure

The HTML dashboard includes:

1. **Executive Summary** - Week's most significant developments
2. **Strategic Highlights** - Major achievements and milestones
3. **Partnership Portfolio** - Status of key relationships
4. **Operations Update** - Team, projects, and capacity
5. **Financial Metrics** - Revenue, pipeline, key indicators
6. **Decisions Required** - Items needing leadership attention
7. **Next Week Preview** - Upcoming priorities and meetings

## Quality Standards

- Publication-quality HTML with Chart.js visualizations
- Significance scoring (distinguish routine from strategic)
- Every statement grounded in actual data
- Strategic context for all developments
- Board-ready presentation quality

## Timeline

Plan for 15-25 minutes processing time for a complete brief.
