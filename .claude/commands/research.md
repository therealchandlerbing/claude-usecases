# Deep Research on Topic

Conduct comprehensive, academic-quality research using the **Open Deep Research Team** skill.

## Instructions

Invoke the `open-deep-research-team` skill for multi-agent research on the specified topic.

## Research Modes

**Express Mode** (10-20 min)
- Quick overview and key findings
- Best for: "Quick research on...", time-constrained requests

**Full Pipeline** (50-85 min)
- Comprehensive, academic-quality analysis
- Best for: "Deep dive into...", "Literature review on..."

**Specialist Focus** (20-35 min)
- Domain-specific expertise (academic, technical, or data)
- Best for: "Academic research on...", "Technical analysis of..."

**Iterative Mode** (30-120+ min)
- Progressive refinement across multiple rounds
- Best for: Complex evolving topics

## Research Agents Available

- **Academic Researcher** - Scholarly sources, peer-reviewed literature
- **Technical Researcher** - Code, implementations, technical docs
- **Data Analyst** - Quantitative analysis, statistics, trends
- **Research Synthesizer** - Consolidate findings across perspectives
- **Report Generator** - Create comprehensive final reports

## Output Format

Deliver comprehensive research report with:
- Executive summary
- Methodology and sources
- Key findings by perspective
- Evidence quality assessment
- Recommendations and next steps
- Full citations

## Usage

Specify topic and optional parameters:
- `/research [topic]` - Default full pipeline
- Mention "quick" for express mode
- Mention "technical" or "academic" for specialist focus
