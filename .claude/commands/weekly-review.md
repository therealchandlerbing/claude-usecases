# Weekly Review & Planning

**Workflow Template**: End-of-week review and next-week preparation

---

## Workflow Steps

Execute the following skills in sequence:

### Step 1: Generate Weekly Intelligence Brief
**Skill**: `executive-intelligence-dashboard`

Compile the week's strategic developments:
- Partnership portfolio updates
- Project completions and milestones
- Team capacity and health
- Financial metrics and pipeline
- Key decisions made

### Step 2: Update Project Tracker
**Skill**: `360-executive-project-tracker`

Refresh project status across all sources:
- Pull latest from Asana, Gmail, Calendar, Drive
- Identify new blockers or risks
- Update completion percentages
- Flag items needing attention next week

### Step 3: Pipeline Health Check
**Skill**: `sales-automator`

Review sales and partnership pipeline:
- Deals that progressed this week
- Stuck deals needing intervention
- New opportunities identified
- Follow-ups due next week

### Step 4: Synthesize & Plan
**Skill**: `ceo-advisor`

Create next-week priorities:
- Top 3 strategic priorities
- Key meetings to prepare for
- Decisions needed
- Risk mitigation actions

---

## Output Deliverables

1. **Weekly Impact Brief** (HTML Dashboard)
   - Publication-quality executive summary
   - Interactive charts and metrics

2. **Project Status Dashboard** (Excel + HTML)
   - Updated tracker with all projects
   - Blocker alerts and risk flags

3. **Pipeline Report** (Summary)
   - Deal status and forecast
   - Required follow-up actions

4. **Next Week Priorities** (Markdown)
   - Strategic focus areas
   - Key preparation notes

---

## Execution Instructions

1. Run weekly brief generation (15-20 min)
2. Update project tracker from all sources (10-15 min)
3. Pull pipeline status and identify stuck deals (5-10 min)
4. Synthesize into next-week planning doc (5-10 min)
5. Review outputs and adjust priorities

---

## Scheduling Recommendation

**Best Time**: Friday afternoon or Monday morning
**Duration**: 45-60 minutes total
**Frequency**: Weekly

---

## Customization Options

**Quick Mode** (25 min):
- Weekly brief + priorities only

**Standard Mode** (45 min):
- Brief + tracker + priorities

**Comprehensive Mode** (60-75 min):
- All four steps with deep analysis
