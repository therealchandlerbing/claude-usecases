# AI Ethics Assessment

Conduct AI ethics evaluation using the **AI Ethics Advisor** skill.

## Instructions

Invoke the `ai-ethics-advisor` skill for bias assessment, fairness evaluation, and responsible AI implementation guidance.

## Assessment Tiers

**Tier 1: Rapid Ethics Screen** (15-30 min)
- Quick assessment for low-risk systems
- Early-stage development review

**Tier 2: Standard Ethics Review** (2-4 hours)
- Medium-risk systems with human oversight
- Pilot programs before scaling

**Tier 3: Comprehensive Ethics Audit** (1-2 days)
- High-risk AI systems
- Large-scale deployments (>10,000 people)
- Regulatory compliance requirements

## Core Assessment Areas

- **Fairness**: Equitable treatment across demographic groups
- **Transparency**: Explainable AI decision-making
- **Accountability**: Clear responsibility chains and audit trails
- **Privacy & Consent**: Data protection and individual agency
- **Human Agency**: Meaningful human control and override
- **Non-Maleficence**: Direct and indirect harm prevention
- **Inclusion & Access**: Expanding rather than restricting opportunity

## High-Priority Use Cases

**Always use for:**
- Employment, lending, healthcare, criminal justice, education systems
- Systems affecting vulnerable populations
- Facial recognition or biometric systems
- Predictive analytics on people
- Automated decisions with significant consequences

## Regulatory Frameworks

- EU AI Act compliance
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework
- IEEE Ethically Aligned Design
- Industry-specific regulations
