# Sales Outreach Sequence

Generate conversion-optimized outreach using the **Sales Automator** skill.

## Instructions

Invoke the `sales-automator` skill to create relationship-informed sales outreach for the specified prospect.

## Required Information

Provide:
1. **Target** - Company name and/or contact name
2. **Opportunity Type** - Partnership, sales, investment, other
3. **Context** - Any known relationship history or connection points

## Workflow

**Phase 1: Intelligence Gathering**
- Research prospect across past conversations, Gmail, Drive, Asana
- Surface relationship history and previous touchpoints
- Identify warmth level and decision-maker access
- Research company, recent news, strategic priorities

**Phase 2: Competitive Analysis**
- Analyze prospect's current solutions
- Identify differentiation opportunities
- Surface relevant case studies and social proof

**Phase 3: Sequence Generation**
- Multi-touch email sequence (3-7 emails)
- Strategic spacing (2-4 weeks)
- Personalization based on research
- A/B subject line options
- CTAs optimized for deal stage

## Output Format

Deliver:
- Complete email sequence (ready to send)
- Research summary with key insights
- Personalization variables
- Recommended timing and cadence
- Follow-up triggers and next steps

## Operating Principles

- **Research first**: Never generic when context is available
- **Value before ask**: Lead with relevant insights
- **Quality over volume**: 10 researched emails > 100 generic ones
- **Multi-touch**: Expect 3-5 touches before conversion
