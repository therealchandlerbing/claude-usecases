# Research to Executive Brief

**Workflow Template**: Deep research → Synthesis → Presentation-ready brief

---

## Workflow Steps

Execute the following skills in sequence:

### Step 1: Comprehensive Research
**Skill**: `open-deep-research-team`

Conduct multi-agent deep research:
- Academic perspective (peer-reviewed sources)
- Technical perspective (implementations, code)
- Data perspective (statistics, trends)
- Market perspective (industry analysis)
- Quality assurance and source validation

### Step 2: Intelligence Extraction
**Skill**: `intelligence-extractor`

Extract actionable intelligence:
- Key findings structured for decision-making
- Stakeholder implications
- Opportunity/risk signals
- Cross-references and connections

### Step 3: Executive Synthesis
**Skill**: `ceo-advisor`

Synthesize for executive consumption:
- So-what analysis
- Strategic implications
- Recommended actions
- Decision framework
- Risk considerations

### Step 4: Visual Presentation
**Skill**: `executive-impact-presentation-generator`

Create presentation-ready materials:
- Executive brief document
- Presentation deck
- Data visualizations
- Key takeaways summary

### Step 5: Design Polish
**Skill**: `design-director`

Elevate to board-ready quality:
- Professional typography
- Visual hierarchy
- Brand consistency
- Accessibility compliance

---

## Required Input

Provide:
1. **Research Topic** - What do you need to understand?
2. **Purpose** - Decision to make, strategy to inform, etc.
3. **Audience** - Board, investors, leadership team, etc.
4. **Depth Level** - Quick overview vs. comprehensive analysis
5. **Key Questions** - Specific questions to answer
6. **Timeline** - When do you need this?

---

## Output Deliverables

1. **Research Report** (Markdown)
   - Comprehensive findings
   - Source citations
   - Confidence levels
   - Knowledge gaps

2. **Intelligence Summary** (JSON + Markdown)
   - Structured key findings
   - Actionable insights
   - Decision-relevant data

3. **Executive Brief** (1-2 pages)
   - Bottom-line-up-front
   - Key findings
   - Implications
   - Recommendations

4. **Presentation Deck** (HTML/PDF)
   - Visual summary
   - Key data points
   - Discussion slides

5. **One-Page Summary** (PDF)
   - At-a-glance overview
   - Print-ready format

---

## Execution Instructions

1. Clarify research questions and scope
2. Run comprehensive research (30-60 min)
3. Extract and structure intelligence (15-20 min)
4. Synthesize for executive audience (15-20 min)
5. Create presentation materials (20-30 min)
6. Apply design polish (15-20 min)
7. Review and refine

---

## Research Depth Options

**Express Mode** (45 min):
- Quick research + 1-page brief
- Best for: Time-sensitive decisions

**Standard Mode** (90 min):
- Full research + brief + deck
- Best for: Most executive decisions

**Comprehensive Mode** (2-3 hours):
- Deep research + all deliverables + citations
- Best for: Board presentations, strategic planning
