# Quick Start: 360 Newsletter Generator

**Generate your first executive brief in 5 minutes.**

---

## Step 1: Choose Your Format (30 seconds)

**Executive Brief** (confidential intelligence):
```
Generate executive brief for last week
```

**General Newsletter** (stakeholder updates):
```
Generate newsletter for last week
```

---

## Step 2: Make Your Request (30 seconds)

### Basic Request

```
Generate newsletter for last week
```

### With Specific Dates

```
Create newsletter for November 11-17
```

### For Board Meeting

```
Generate November newsletter for board meeting
```

### Partnership Focus

```
Newsletter for SpacePlan partnership only, last 30 days
```

---

## Step 3: What Happens Next

1. **Date confirmation** (5 seconds)
   - Verify the reporting period

2. **Data collection** (30-40 seconds)
   - Asana: Project progress, tasks, milestones
   - Gmail: Communications, announcements
   - Google Drive: Reports, documents

3. **Content analysis** (10 seconds)
   - Significance scoring (0-100)
   - Lead story selection
   - Section assignment

4. **HTML generation** (10 seconds)
   - Professional dashboard layout
   - Interactive charts
   - Collapsible sections

**Total time: ~60 seconds**

---

## Step 4: Review and Customize

After receiving your newsletter, you can request:

```
Expand the partnerships section

Add a chart showing team capacity

Change the lead story to [specific item]

Focus more on operations, less on strategy

Include more financial metrics
```

---

## What You'll Receive

### Executive Brief Includes:

**Executive Snapshot** (4 cards)
- People status
- Revenue position
- Partnership health
- Key risks

**6 Color-Coded Sections**
- Partnerships & Ecosystem (blue)
- Programs & Innovation (teal)
- Impact & Outcomes (purple)
- Operations & Capacity (green)
- Strategic Horizon (orange)

**Interactive Elements**
- 4+ charts (bar, pie, radar, line)
- Metric cards with trends
- Status badges
- Timeline with week/2-week views
- Collapsible sections

**Action Items**
- Flagged tasks with owners
- Due dates and priorities

## Tips for Better Results

**Be specific about dates:**
```
Generate newsletter for November 1-15
```

**Specify audience for appropriate content:**
```
Create board brief (includes governance items)
Create team update (more operational detail)
```

**Request financial depth when needed:**
```
Generate executive brief with financial metrics
```

**Focus on specific areas:**
```
Newsletter focused on partnerships only
```
