# 360 Newsletter Generator - Quick Start

## One-Line Summary
Generate professional, publication-style newsletters and executive briefs by automatically synthesizing updates from Asana, Gmail, and Google Drive into interactive HTML dashboards with data visualizations.

**Simple commands:**
```

```

**With specifics:**
```

```

### Step 2: Auto-Generation (60-90 seconds)

System automatically:
1. Connects to Asana, Gmail, Drive (5 sec)
2. Collects data from date range (30-40 sec)
3. Analyzes and scores content (10 sec)
4. Generates HTML dashboard with charts (10 sec)
5. Delivers interactive artifact (60-90 sec total)

### Step 3: Review & Share

You receive:
- Interactive HTML dashboard (self-contained)
- Optional PDF export
- Content organized by section
- Charts with trend data

## Two Output Types

### 1. Executive Brief (Confidential)

**Audience:** Leadership, board, investors
**Content:** Financial data, HR updates, strategic decisions
**Sensitivity:** Executive-level confidential
**Use when:** Board meetings, investor updates, leadership briefings

### 2. General Newsletter (Public)

**Audience:** Stakeholders, partners, public
**Content:** High-level updates, impact stories, achievements
**Sensitivity:** Public-appropriate information
**Use when:** Monthly stakeholder updates, partnership communications

**Note:** Both use identical professional design - distinction is content only.

## Newsletter Structure

### 6 Sections

1. **Executive Summary** - Top 3-4 highlights (gradient blue banner)
2. **Partnerships & Ecosystem** - Pipeline status, agreements, relationships
3. **Programs & Innovation** - Projects, assessments, client work
4. **Impact & Outcomes** - Metrics, testimonials, community outcomes
5. **Operations & Capacity** - Team, infrastructure, financial sustainability
6. **Strategic Horizon** - Upcoming events, deadlines, board priorities

### Visual Components

**Charts (4 minimum):**
- Partnership pipeline by stage (bar chart)
- Project distribution by region (pie chart)
- Impact metrics trend (line chart)
- Revenue/sustainability trajectory (line chart)

**Metric Cards:**
- Partnership value/count
- Active projects
- Social impact numbers
- Team capacity
- Financial runway

## Content Prioritization

**Significance Scoring (0-100):**
- Strategic importance: 40 points
- Stakeholder impact: 30 points
- Urgency/timeliness: 20 points
- Completeness: 10 points

**Lead Story Selection:**
- Highest significance score
- Passes completeness threshold
- Has actionable context
