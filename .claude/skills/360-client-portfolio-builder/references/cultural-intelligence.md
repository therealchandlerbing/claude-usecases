# Cultural Intelligence - 360 Client Portfolio Builder

**Version:** 1.0.0
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-16
**Purpose:** International and cultural adaptation guidance for portfolio pages

---

## Overview

360 works with ventures across geographies and cultures. Portfolio pages must adapt to local contexts while maintaining quality and 360 brand coherence.

**Key Principle:** Respect cultural context without stereotyping or oversimplifying.

---

## Brazil & Latin America

### Cultural Context

**Key Values:**
- Relationships and trust (personalismo)
- Warmth and human connection
- Community and collective benefit
- Patience with longer timelines
- Respect for hierarchy and experience

**Business Culture:**
- Relationship-first, then transaction
- Personal connections matter deeply
- Face-to-face interaction valued
- Formality in initial engagements
- More flexibility with time and process

### Content Adaptations

**Tone Adjustments:**

North American (Direct):
> "We deliver 40% cost savings through AI-powered optimization."

Brazilian (Relationship-Oriented):
> "Em parceria com comunidades locais, criamos soluções que reduzem custos em 40% enquanto fortalecem laços comunitários."
> (In partnership with local communities, we create solutions that reduce costs by 40% while strengthening community bonds.)

**Framing Patterns:**

**Emphasize:**
- Partnership and collaboration ("em parceria", "junto com")
- Community benefit ("benefício comunitário", "impacto social")
- Long-term relationships ("relacionamento duradouro")
- Trust and credibility ("confiança", "credibilidade")
- Local context and adaptation ("adaptado ao contexto local")

**De-emphasize:**
- Aggressive growth or disruption
- Individual achievement over collective
- Speed at expense of relationship
- Purely transactional language

**Example Hero Section:**

North American Version:
> "EcoGrid delivers affordable solar energy to off-grid communities, achieving 40% cost reduction through AI-powered micro-grids."

Brazilian-Adapted Version (Portuguese):
> "EcoGrid: Energia Solar Acessível para Comunidades"
> "Em parceria com cooperativas locais, levamos energia limpa e acessível para comunidades rurais, construindo um futuro sustentável juntos."

> (EcoGrid: Accessible Solar Energy for Communities)
> (In partnership with local cooperatives, we bring clean and accessible energy to rural communities, building a sustainable future together.)

### Visual Adaptations

**Color Palette:**
- Warmer tones (reds, oranges, yellows) vs. cool blues
- Vibrant, saturated colors vs. muted/pastel
- Earth tones connecting to nature
- Green/yellow for Brazilian context (national colors)

**Imagery:**
- Real people (not stock photos) in local context
- Community gatherings and group activities
- Multi-generational representation
- Natural settings and landscapes
- Authentic Brazilian/LATAM settings

**Typography:**
- Slightly warmer, more human fonts
- Less rigid geometric sans-serifs
- Comfortable reading size (avoid tiny text)

### Partnership Framing

**De-emphasize:**
- 360 as "expert" solving local problems
- Technology-first framing
- Charity or aid narrative

**Emphasize:**
- 360 as partner and facilitator
- Local knowledge and leadership
- Co-creation and collaboration
- Mutual learning and growth

**Example Partnership Section:**

```markdown
## Parceria com 360 Social Impact Studios

A [Nome do Cliente] trabalha em parceria com a 360 Social Impact Studios para acelerar o impacto social e fortalecer conexões no ecossistema de inovação. Através de processos colaborativos do Vianeo, desenvolvemos juntos estratégias que respeitam o contexto local enquanto trazem melhores práticas globais.

"A 360 não chegou com soluções prontas. Eles ouviram, aprenderam com nossa realidade, e juntos construímos algo que realmente funciona aqui." — [Nome], Fundador(a)

Conheça mais sobre a abordagem colaborativa da 360 →
```

(Translation: [Client Name] works in partnership with 360 Social Impact Studios to accelerate social impact and strengthen connections in the innovation ecosystem. Through collaborative Vianeo processes, we developed together strategies that respect local context while bringing global best practices.

"360 didn't arrive with ready-made solutions. They listened, learned from our reality, and together we built something that really works here." — [Name], Founder

Learn more about 360's collaborative approach →)

### Language Considerations

**Portuguese for Brazilian Audiences:**

**Critical Sections to Translate:**
- Hero section (heading, tagline)
- Navigation menu
- Section headers
- Call-to-action buttons
- Contact information
- 360 partnership section

**Can Remain in English (if needed):**
- Detailed technical descriptions
- Metrics and data (with Portuguese labels)
- Quoted testimonials in English (with translation)

**Bilingual Approach:**

```html
<!-- Hero Section - Bilingual -->
<section class="hero">
    <div class="hero-content">
        <span class="language-toggle">
            <a href="#" data-lang="pt" class="active">Português</a> |
            <a href="#" data-lang="en">English</a>
        </span>

        <h1 data-pt="Energia Solar Acessível para Comunidades"
            data-en="Affordable Solar Energy for Communities">
            Energia Solar Acessível para Comunidades
        </h1>

        <p class="tagline"
           data-pt="Em parceria com cooperativas locais, construindo um futuro sustentável"
           data-en="In partnership with local cooperatives, building a sustainable future">
            Em parceria com cooperativas locais, construindo um futuro sustentável
        </p>
    </div>
</section>
```

**Portuguese Translation Quality:**

- Use Brazilian Portuguese (not European Portuguese)
- Avoid literal translation (adapt for cultural resonance)
- Consider regional variations (use neutral Brazilian Portuguese)
- Hire native speaker for final review

- Data-driven decision making

## General International Adaptations

### Multi-Language Considerations

**Minimal Translation (Budget-Conscious):**
- Hero section (heading, tagline)
- Navigation menu
- Section headers
- Call-to-action
- Contact information

**Full Translation (Recommended):**
- All content
- Meta tags and SEO
- Alt text for images
- Form labels and buttons

**Hybrid Approach:**
- Primary content in local language
- Technical details available in English
- Metrics and data with localized labels
- Toggle between languages

### Currency and Units

**Localize:**
- Currency symbols and formatting
- Date formats (DD/MM/YYYY vs. MM/DD/YYYY)
- Number formatting (1,000 vs. 1.000)
- Measurement units (metric vs. imperial)

**Example:**

US:
> "$250K in pilot revenue"
> "6 months"

Europe:
> "€230K in pilot revenue"
> "6 months"

Brazil:
> "R$ 1,2M em receita piloto"
> "6 meses"

### Time Zones and Contact

**Show Local Time Awareness:**

Instead of:
> "Contact us: available 9am-5pm EST"

Use:
> "Contact us: available Monday-Friday, São Paulo business hours (GMT-3)"

Or:
> "Contact us: We respond within 24 hours across all time zones"

### Cultural Imagery Considerations

**Avoid:**
- Stock photos of business people pointing at screens
- Western-centric imagery for non-Western audiences
- Stereotypical or culturally insensitive representations
- Images that don't reflect actual team or geography

**Use:**
- Authentic photos from actual projects
- Local team members and partners
- Real customers or beneficiaries (with permission)
- Culturally appropriate settings

## Accessibility Across Cultures

### Reading Direction

**Right-to-Left (RTL) Languages (Arabic, Hebrew, etc.):**

If supporting RTL languages:
- Mirror entire layout
- Flip navigation direction
- Adjust text alignment
- Test carefully in target language

**Implementation:**

```html
<html lang="ar" dir="rtl">
```

```css
[dir="rtl"] .layout-60-40 {
    grid-template-columns: 40% 60%; /* Flip for RTL */
}
```

### Color Meaning

**Cultural Color Associations:**

| Color | Western | China | India | Middle East |
|-------|---------|-------|-------|-------------|
| Red | Passion, danger | Luck, celebration | Purity, fertility | Danger, caution |
| White | Purity, peace | Death, mourning | Peace, purity | Purity, mourning |
| Green | Nature, growth | Health, prosperity | Fertility, luck | Islam, paradise |
| Blue | Trust, calm | Immortality | Krishna, mourning | Protection, spirituality |
| Yellow | Happiness, caution | Royalty, power | Sacred, learning | Happiness, prosperity |

**Recommendation:** Use neutral colors or research cultural significance before finalizing palette for specific regions.

### Symbols and Icons

**Be Cautious With:**
- Hand gestures (meanings vary widely)
- Religious symbols
- National flags or politics
- Animals (sacred, taboo, or symbolic)

**Safe Icons:**
- Universal symbols (play, pause, search, etc.)
- Simple geometric shapes
- Abstract representations
- Tested across cultures

## Common Cultural Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake 1: Direct Translation Without Cultural Adaptation

**Problem:**
> Translating U.S.-style aggressive growth language directly into Portuguese sounds tone-deaf.

**Solution:**
> Adapt tone and framing for cultural context, don't just translate words.

---

### ❌ Mistake 2: Western-Centric Case Studies

**Problem:**
> Using only U.S./European examples for Latin American or African audiences.

**Solution:**
> Include local case studies, testimonials, and partnerships relevant to target geography.

---

### ❌ Mistake 3: Ignoring Local Regulations

**Problem:**
> Not mentioning GDPR compliance for European audiences or LGPD for Brazil.

**Solution:**
> Explicitly address relevant regulatory frameworks and data protections.

---

### ❌ Mistake 4: One-Size-Fits-All Language

**Problem:**
> Using "global English" that doesn't resonate with any specific culture.

**Solution:**
> Create region-specific versions with culturally adapted content.

---

### ❌ Mistake 5: Stereotyping or Oversimplifying

**Problem:**
> "Latin Americans care more about relationships" → using overly casual tone that loses credibility.

**Solution:**
> Balance cultural intelligence with professionalism. Adapt, don't stereotype.
