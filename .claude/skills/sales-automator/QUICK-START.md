## Outreach Sequence Templates

### Cold Outreach (New Prospects)
**5-touch sequence over 3 weeks:**
- **Email 1** (Day 0): Insight/value, no ask
- **Email 2** (Day 3): Case study/proof, soft ask
- **Email 3** (Day 7): Direct ask with urgency
- **Email 4** (Day 14): Breakup/"last chance"
- **Email 5** (Day 21): Different angle (if no response)

**Response rate:** 15-25%

### Warm Follow-Up (Existing Relationship)
**3-touch sequence over 2 weeks:**
- **Email 1** (Day 0): Reference past conversation, new value
- **Email 2** (Day 5): Specific proposal/next step
- **Email 3** (Day 12): Timeline urgency, alternate options

**Response rate:** 30-45%

### Re-Engagement (Stuck Deals)
**4-touch sequence over 10 days:**
- **Email 1** (Day 0): Check-in, new insight
- **Email 2** (Day 3): Permission to close loop
- **Email 3** (Day 7): Breakup email
- **Email 4** (Day 10): Hail Mary (different exec, different angle)

**Response rate:** 10-20% (reactivation from "dead")

## Operating Principles

### 1. Relationship-First, Not Template-First
Begin with intelligence gathering. Never send generic emails when you can pull context.

### 2. Value Before Ask
Lead with insights, data, case studies. Demonstrate relevance before asking for time.

### 3. Multi-Touch, Not One-and-Done
Cold emails rarely convert on first touch. Build familiarity over 2-4 weeks.

### 4. Data-Driven Optimization
Track what works, iterate on what doesn't. Every campaign generates learnings.

### 5. Strategic, Not Scalable
Send 10 researched emails at 30% conversion vs. 100 generic at 2%.

## Asana Pipeline Setup

### Required Custom Fields:
- **Deal Stage**: Initial Contact, Qualification, Proposal Sent, Negotiation, Closed-Won, Closed-Lost
- **Deal Value**: Number (currency)
- **Close Date**: Date
- **Lead Source**: Cold Outreach, Warm Intro, Inbound, Partner Referral
- **Lead Score**: Hot, Warm, Cold
- **Last Touch Date**: Date
- **Next Action**: Text (what's the next step)

### Asana Project Structure:
- **Section 1**: Initial Contact (new prospects)
- **Section 2**: Qualification (qualified leads)
- **Section 3**: Proposal Sent (active deals)
- **Section 4**: Negotiation (closing)
- **Section 5**: Closed-Won (won deals)
- **Section 6**: Closed-Lost (lost deals, with reason)
