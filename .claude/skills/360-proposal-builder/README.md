# 360 Proposal Builder

Generate executive-grade proposals for 360 Social Impact Studios' innovation transformation services.

## Overview

This skill creates customized proposals with proper GenIP attribution, cultural considerations, and strategic positioning across three service tracks:

- **Innovation Assessment Track** - Portfolio triage and decision clarity
- **Business Design Track** - Venture building and execution
- **Leadership & Culture Track** - Capability building and transformation

## Quick Start

1. Provide discovery information about the client
2. Specify their innovation challenges
3. Indicate budget range and timeline
4. Receive customized proposal with proper service selection

## Key Features

- **GenIP Attribution Compliance** - Ensures proper crediting of methodology partnerships
- **Service Portfolio Mastery** - Accurate pricing, deliverables, and positioning
- **Cultural Adaptation** - Adjusts for clients across global regions
- **Template Customization** - Transforms templates into client-specific proposals

**Venture Building:**

**Leadership Development:**
"Create a proposal for a government agency wanting to train 20 innovation practitioners over 6 months."

## Critical Reminders

1. **Always attribute GenIP correctly** - Use "Our methodology from GenIP" pattern
2. **Never use em dashes** - Restructure sentences instead
3. **Don't skip discovery** - Client context makes proposals win
4. **Position as strategic partner** - Not a vendor delivering services
