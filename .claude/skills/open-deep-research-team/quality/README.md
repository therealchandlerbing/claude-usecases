# Quality Assurance Framework

This directory contains quality assurance tools, checklists, and frameworks for the Open Deep Research Team.

## Contents

- **[research-quality-checklist.md](research-quality-checklist.md)** - Comprehensive quality checklist for all research
- **[validation-framework.md](validation-framework.md)** - Multi-level validation framework
- **[confidence-scoring-guide.md](confidence-scoring-guide.md)** - Detailed confidence scoring methodology

## Purpose

Quality assurance ensures that every research project meets high standards for:
- Academic rigor
- Source credibility
- Comprehensive coverage
- Transparent limitations
- Actionable insights

## Usage

1. Review **research-quality-checklist.md** before starting research
2. Apply **validation-framework.md** at each research phase
3. Use **confidence-scoring-guide.md** when assigning confidence scores

## Quality Standards

All research should achieve:
- ✅ Average confidence score > 0.7
- ✅ 80%+ sources from Tier 1-2
- ✅ All research questions addressed
- ✅ Contradictions explicitly analyzed
- ✅ Limitations transparently acknowledged
- ✅ Citations complete and properly formatted
