### Level 3: Advanced (Maximum Control)

**When to use**: Complex research with specific requirements.

**Template:**
```

- [Area 2]
- [Area 3]

**Source Preferences:**

- [e.g., Include code repositories]
- [e.g., Industry reports from major analysts]

**Geographic Scope:**
- [e.g., Global, North America, EU, specific countries]

**Output Requirements:**
- Format: [Academic report | Business report | Technical docs | Executive brief]
- Length: [Brief | Standard | Comprehensive]
- Include: [Contradictions analysis, recommendations, etc.]

**Special Considerations:**
- [Any unique requirements]
```

**Example:**
```
Please conduct comprehensive research on federated learning for healthcare with the following configuration:

**Focus Areas:**
- Privacy-preserving techniques and HIPAA compliance
- Recent academic advances (2022-2024)
- Production implementations in clinical settings
- Performance benchmarks vs. centralized learning

**Source Preferences:**
- Peer-reviewed journals and conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, medical AI)
- GitHub repositories with 500+ stars
- Healthcare industry case studies
- FDA/regulatory guidance documents

**Geographic Scope:**
- United States (primary)
- European Union (GDPR considerations)

**Output Requirements:**
- Format: Business report with technical appendix
- Length: Comprehensive (60+ minutes)
- Include: Full contradiction analysis, implementation roadmap

**Special Considerations:**
- Emphasize regulatory compliance aspects
- Compare against centralized alternatives
- Include security threat model analysis
```

**What you get:**
- Precisely targeted research
- Custom source filtering
- Tailored output format
- All special requirements addressed
- 75-95 minutes for comprehensive analysis

## ‚ö° Quick Reference: Common Research Patterns

### Pattern 1: Technology Evaluation

**Use case**: Comparing tools, frameworks, or platforms

**Template:**
```
Please conduct comprehensive research comparing [OPTION A], [OPTION B], and [OPTION C] for [USE CASE], focusing on:
- Features and capabilities
- Performance benchmarks
- Production readiness and case studies
- Community support and ecosystem
- Cost and licensing
- Output as technical documentation
```

**Example:**
```
Please conduct comprehensive research comparing Kubernetes, Docker Swarm, and Nomad for microservices orchestration, focusing on:
- Features and capabilities
- Performance benchmarks
- Production readiness and case studies
- Community support and ecosystem
- Cost and licensing
- Output as technical documentation
```

**Time**: 55-70 minutes

---

### Pattern 2: Market Research

**Use case**: Understanding market size, trends, and opportunities

**Template:**
```
Please conduct comprehensive market research on [MARKET/SECTOR], focusing on:
- Current market size and growth rates
- Key players and competitive landscape
- Industry trends (last 3-5 years)
- Future projections (3-5 years)
- Investment and M&A activity
- Regional breakdown (if relevant)
- Output as business report
```

**Example:**
```
Please conduct comprehensive market research on AI-powered customer service automation, focusing on:
- Current market size and growth rates (2020-2024)
- Key players (incumbents vs. startups)
- Technology trends (LLMs, multimodal AI)
- Future projections (2024-2028)
- Enterprise adoption patterns
- Regional breakdown (North America, Europe, Asia)
- Output as executive presentation
```

**Time**: 60-75 minutes

---

### Pattern 3: Academic Literature Review

**Use case**: Survey of academic research on a topic

**Template:**
```
Please conduct an academic literature review on [RESEARCH TOPIC], focusing on:
- Seminal papers and foundational work
- Recent advances ([TIME PERIOD])
- Methodologies and approaches
- Research gaps and future directions
- Key researchers and institutions
- Citation network analysis
- Output as academic report
```

**Example:**
```
Please conduct an academic literature review on graph neural networks for molecular property prediction, focusing on:
- Seminal papers (2015-2020)
- Recent advances (2021-2024)
- Architectural innovations and methodologies
- Benchmark datasets and performance
- Research gaps and open problems
- Key researchers and research groups
- Output as academic paper format
```

**Time**: 65-85 minutes

---

### Pattern 4: Competitive Intelligence

**Use case**: Understanding competitors and their strategies

**Template:**
```
Please conduct competitive intelligence research on [TOPIC/MARKET], analyzing:
- [Competitor 1]
- [Competitor 2]
- [Competitor 3]
- Focus on: product features, pricing, market position, technology approach
- Include: strengths, weaknesses, market share, recent developments
- Output as business report
```

**Example:**
```
Please conduct competitive intelligence research on AI coding assistants, analyzing:
- GitHub Copilot
- Amazon CodeWhisperer
- Tabnine
- Replit Ghostwriter
- Focus on: features, model capabilities, pricing, IDE integration, accuracy
- Include: strengths, weaknesses, user adoption, enterprise features
- Output as competitive analysis report
```

**Time**: 55-70 minutes

---

### Pattern 5: Trend Analysis

**Use case**: Understanding how a field is evolving

**Template:**
```
Please conduct trend analysis research on [TOPIC], examining:
- Historical evolution ([START YEAR] to present)
- Current state and major developments
- Emerging patterns and inflection points
- Future projections and scenarios
- Key drivers and inhibitors
- Output as business report with data visualizations
```

**Example:**
```
Please conduct trend analysis research on remote work adoption, examining:
- Historical evolution (2015-2024)
- COVID-19 impact and aftermath
- Technology enablers (video, collaboration, security)
- Productivity research findings
- Real estate and geographic impacts
- Future of work projections (2024-2030)
- Output as executive presentation with charts
```

**Time**: 60-80 minutes

## üéØ Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Be Specific About Your Goal

**Instead of:**
```
Research AI in healthcare
```

**Try:**
```
Research AI for medical imaging diagnostics, focusing on FDA-approved solutions,
accuracy benchmarks, and hospital adoption patterns (2022-2024)
```

**Why**: Specific goals = more focused, valuable research.

---

### Tip 2: Specify Source Preferences

**Add source guidance:**
```
- Prioritize peer-reviewed sources
- Include code repositories for technical analysis
- Focus on industry reports from Gartner, Forrester
- Include case studies from Fortune 500 implementations
```

**Why**: You get the sources most relevant for your decision.

---

### Tip 3: Request Contradiction Analysis

**Add this line:**
```
Please include explicit contradiction analysis for contentious findings
```

**Why**: Understanding disagreements prevents one-sided conclusions.

---

### Tip 4: Ask for Recommendations

**Add this:**
```
Include actionable recommendations based on findings
```

**Why**: Moves from insights to action.

---

### Tip 5: Specify Your Audience

**Add context:**
```
This research is for [technical team | executive board | investors | academic submission]
```

**Why**: Output will be tailored to audience expertise and priorities.

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Q: Can I request multiple research projects at once?
**A**: Yes! You can queue them or run them in sequence. For parallel execution, be explicit:
```
Please conduct three separate research projects:
1. [Topic A] - Express mode
2. [Topic B] - Standard depth
3. [Topic C] - Standard depth
```

---

### Q: What if sources are behind paywalls?
**A**: The research will note paywall limitations and use available abstracts, preprints, and alternative sources. Quality assessment will reflect limited access.

---

### Q: Can I build on previous research?
**A**: Absolutely! Reference past research:
```
Building on our previous research on [TOPIC], please conduct deeper analysis on [SPECIFIC ASPECT]
```

---

### Q: What if my question is unclear?
**A**: The Query Clarifier will ask targeted questions to refine your request. This improves final output quality.

---

### Q: Can I get updates during long research?
**A**: Yes! TodoWrite integration provides real-time progress updates showing which phase and agents are active.

---

### Q: What if I need research urgently?
**A**: Request Express mode:
```
Please conduct quick research on [TOPIC] - I need results in 15 minutes
```

Note: Quality is reduced for speed. Use for preliminary research only.
