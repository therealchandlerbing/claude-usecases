## Quick Reference

### Source Tiers
- **Tier 1**: Peer-reviewed journals (IF > 5), official docs, government data
- **Tier 2**: Peer-reviewed journals (IF 2-5), preprints, maintained OSS
- **Tier 3**: Expert blogs, industry publications, conference proceedings
- **Tier 4**: General news, social media (use with caution)

### Confidence Levels
- **0.9-1.0**: Very high confidence (multiple Tier 1 sources, consensus)
- **0.7-0.89**: High confidence (good sources, clear evidence weight)
- **0.5-0.69**: Moderate confidence (mixed sources, some contradictions)
- **0.3-0.49**: Low confidence (limited sources, significant contradictions)
- **0.0-0.29**: Very low confidence (speculative, poor sources)

### Citation Formats
- **Academic**: APA 7th edition (default for academic reports)
- **Business**: Endnotes with abbreviated citations
- **Technical**: Inline links with titles
- **Executive**: Minimal inline citations, full bibliography in appendix
