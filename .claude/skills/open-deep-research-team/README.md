## üìä Quality Metrics

Every research project is evaluated on:

### Source Quality
- **Tier 1**: Peer-reviewed journals, official documentation, government databases
- **Tier 2**: Preprints, well-maintained open source, industry reports
- **Tier 3**: Expert blogs, reputable news, conference proceedings
- **Target**: 80%+ Tier 1-2 sources

### Coverage Depth
- **Express**: 10-20 sources
- **Standard**: 30-50 sources
- **Comprehensive**: 50-100+ sources

### Confidence Scoring
- **0.9-1.0**: Very high confidence (multiple high-quality sources, strong consensus)
- **0.7-0.89**: High confidence (good sources, clear weight of evidence)
- **0.5-0.69**: Moderate confidence (mixed sources, some contradictions)
- **0.3-0.49**: Low confidence (limited sources, significant contradictions)
- **0.0-0.29**: Very low confidence (speculative, poor sources)

**Target**: Average confidence score > 0.7 for comprehensive research

### Research Completeness
- ‚úÖ All research questions addressed
- ‚úÖ Multiple perspectives included
- ‚úÖ Contradictions identified and analyzed
- ‚úÖ Limitations explicitly acknowledged
- ‚úÖ Recommendations actionable
- ‚úÖ Citations complete and accurate

## üéì Best Practices

### Maximize Research Quality

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Provide specific research objectives
- ‚úÖ Indicate preferred source types
- ‚úÖ Specify time constraints upfront
- ‚úÖ Request specific output format
- ‚úÖ Ask for contradiction analysis
- ‚úÖ Include relevant context

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Rush complex research
- ‚ùå Accept low confidence for critical decisions
- ‚ùå Ignore acknowledged limitations
- ‚ùå Skip clarification when unclear
- ‚ùå Overlook contradictory evidence

### Effective Query Patterns

**Strong Queries Include:**
1. Specific topic or question
2. Focus areas or subtopics
3. Time frame (if relevant)
4. Geographic scope (if relevant)
5. Intended use or audience
6. Output format preference

**Example**:
```
Research the current state of autonomous vehicle technology, focusing on:
- Sensor fusion techniques (academic research 2022-2024)
- Regulatory challenges in US and EU
- Market projections 2024-2030
- Key players and their approaches
- Output as executive presentation for board
```
