## Scenario 2: Board Meeting Preparation

### Context
CEO has a board meeting in 3 days and needs comprehensive preparation including member analysis, predicted questions, and presentation strategy.

### Invocation
```
"Prepare me for the board meeting on Thursday"
```

### Example Output

```
## Board Meeting Preparation - Thursday, November 21

### Board Composition Analysis

**Sarah Chen (Chair)**
- Style: Analytical | Priority: Growth metrics
- Recent concern: Burn rate trajectory
- Likely questions: "What's the path to profitability?" "How does AI investment affect runway?"
- Preparation: Bring detailed unit economics analysis, scenario models

**Michael Torres (Audit Chair)**
- Style: Analytical | Priority: Risk and compliance
- Recent concern: Customer concentration
- Likely questions: "Top 5 customer exposure?" "What's our D&O coverage?"
- Preparation: Customer diversification plan, updated risk register

**Jennifer Walsh (Investor Director)**
- Style: Driver | Priority: Market expansion
- Recent concern: Competitive positioning
- Likely questions: "Market share trajectory?" "Why not expand faster?"
- Preparation: Competitive analysis, expansion timeline with dependencies

### Predicted Hot Topics

1. **AI Investment ROI** (High probability)
   - Concern: $500K investment with 18-month payback
   - Preparation: Show 3 scenarios, customer demand signals, competitive necessity
   - Risk: Appears speculative
   - Mitigation: Tie to specific customer requests and competitive gaps

2. **Cash Management** (Medium probability)
   - Concern: 14 months runway with planned investments
   - Preparation: Show capital allocation scenarios, fundraise optionality
   - Risk: Appears to need funding soon
   - Mitigation: Show path to profitability without funding

3. **Customer Concentration** (High probability)
   - Concern: Top 3 = 35% of revenue
   - Preparation: Diversification pipeline, multi-threading progress
   - Risk: Vulnerability to single loss
   - Mitigation: Show renewal confidence scores, expansion plans

### Narrative Arc

**Opening (5 min)**: Start with customer wins and product momentum
- Key message: "Market is validating our AI direction"
- Supporting data: 3 new enterprise logos, 15% NRR improvement

**Growth Update (10 min)**: Q4 progress and Q1 outlook
- Key message: "On track with improving efficiency"
- Supporting data: Pipeline coverage, sales productivity gains

**Strategic Discussion (15 min)**: AI investment decision
- Key message: "Critical for competitive positioning"
- Supporting data: Customer requests, competitive analysis, risk scenarios

**Financial Review (10 min)**: Cash position and forecasts
- Key message: "Runway sufficient with optionality"
- Supporting data: Scenarios, fundraise timing, path to profitability

**Q&A (20 min)**: Prepared for hot topics

### Materials Checklist

- [ ] Board deck final version (due: Wednesday 5pm)
- [ ] Financial appendix with scenarios
- [ ] Customer health dashboard
- [ ] Competitive intelligence update
- [ ] Risk register update
- [ ] 1-pager executive summary for pre-read

### Day-Of Logistics

- Arrive 30 min early for tech check
- Have backup materials printed
- Water and snacks available
- Phone on silent, EA handling interrupts
```

## Scenario 4: Strategic Planning Session

### Context
CEO wants to facilitate a strategic planning discussion about market expansion into Europe.

### Invocation
```
"Facilitate a strategic planning session on European market expansion"
```

### Example Output

```
## Strategic Planning Session: European Expansion

### Session Setup

**Objective**: Evaluate European market expansion opportunity and determine strategic approach

**Participants**: CEO, CFO, CRO, VP Product, VP Operations

**Duration**: 2 hours

**Agenda**:
1. Market opportunity assessment (30 min)
2. Capability gap analysis (20 min)
3. Option development (30 min)
4. Financial modeling (20 min)
5. Decision and next steps (20 min)

---

### Market Opportunity Assessment

**Market Size & Growth**
- Total addressable market: $2.4B (Europe)
- Serviceable market: $480M (enterprise segment)
- Annual growth: 18%

**Key Markets**
1. **UK** - $150M SAM, English-speaking, regulatory complexity
2. **Germany** - $120M SAM, strong enterprise, data privacy focus
3. **France** - $80M SAM, government sector opportunity

**Competitive Landscape**
- Market Leader Inc: Strong UK presence, weak DACH
- Local Champion: Strong Germany, limited product
- Gap: No clear AI leader in enterprise space

### Capability Gap Analysis

| Capability | Current State | Required State | Gap |
|------------|---------------|----------------|-----|
| Sales | US only | EU team | Major |
| Support | 9-5 PT | 24-hour | Major |
| Compliance | SOC2, HIPAA | GDPR, local | Significant |
| Localization | English | DE, FR | Significant |
| Payments | USD | EUR, local | Moderate |

**Gap Closure Timeline**: 9-12 months for full capability

### Strategic Options

**Option A: Organic Build**
- Investment: $750K over 12 months
- Timeline: Revenue in Q3 2025
- Risk: Slower, execution complexity
- Pros: Full control, higher margins long-term

**Option B: Partnership**
- Investment: $200K + 30% revenue share
- Timeline: Revenue in Q1 2025
- Risk: Partner dependency, margin impact
- Pros: Faster, lower upfront risk

**Option C: Acquisition**
- Investment: $2-5M
- Timeline: Revenue immediate
- Risk: Integration, culture
- Pros: Instant capability, customer base

**Option D: Defer**
- Investment: $0
- Timeline: Revisit in 12 months
- Risk: Competitive window closes
- Pros: Focus on US, preserve capital

### Financial Modeling

**Option A: Organic Build**
| Metric | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Investment | $750K | $300K | $200K |
| Revenue | $200K | $1.2M | $3M |
| Contribution | -$550K | $600K | $2.2M |

**Breakeven**: Month 18
**ROI**: 2.0x by Year 3

**Option B: Partnership**
| Metric | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Investment | $200K | $100K | $100K |
| Revenue | $400K | $1M | $2M |
| Net (after share) | $80K | $600K | $1.3M |

**Breakeven**: Month 8
**ROI**: 1.5x by Year 3

### Recommendation

**Pursue Option B (Partnership) with Option A (Organic) transition plan**

**Rationale**:
1. Fast market entry validates opportunity
2. Lower risk with current runway
3. Learn market before major investment
4. Transition to organic once proven

**Key Success Factors**:
- Right partner selection (local presence, complementary)
- Clear transition path to organic
- Protect key accounts for direct relationship

### Next Steps

1. **This Week**: Identify 3-5 potential partners
2. **Week 2**: Initial partner conversations
3. **Week 4**: Partner evaluation and selection
4. **Week 6**: Term sheet negotiation
5. **Q1 2025**: Partnership launch
```

## Using These Examples

These scenarios demonstrate how the CEO Advisor Team of Experts integrates multiple analytical perspectives to deliver comprehensive, actionable guidance. Each scenario shows:

1. **Clear invocation** - Natural language requests
2. **Structured output** - Scannable, executive-appropriate format
3. **Actionable recommendations** - Specific next steps with timing
4. **Integrated intelligence** - All five experts contributing

Customize these patterns for your specific context, industry, and leadership style.
