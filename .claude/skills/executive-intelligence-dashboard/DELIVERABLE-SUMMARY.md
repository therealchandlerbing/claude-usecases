# Deliverable Summary

**What you get: A publication-quality executive intelligence dashboard.**

## File Specifications

**Format**: Single HTML file (self-contained)
**File Size**: 100-150KB typically
**File Name**: `360-impact-brief-[YYYY-MM-DD].html`

**Dependencies**: External resources loaded from CDN
- Chart.js 4.4.0 (visualizations)
- Font Awesome 6.4.0 (icons)
- Inter font (typography)

**Browser Compatibility**: Modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
**Print Ready**: Optimized print layout for distribution
**Accessibility**: WCAG AA compliant

## Content Sections

### Executive Summary (Blue Banner)

**Contents**:
- Three summary cards (60-80 words each)
- Strategic inflection points
- Partnership momentum highlights
- Team evolution updates

**Purpose**: 60-second strategic overview

### Partnerships & Business Development (Purple)

**Components**:
- Lead story (most significant development)
- Strategic partnership updates (2-4 cards)
- Active client work
- Service development initiatives
- Service package market fit chart
- Action items (3-5 tasks)

### Operations & Team (Green)

**Components**:
- Metrics cards (team size, capacity, competencies)
- Team restructuring updates
- Candidate analysis
- Merger/acquisition integration
- Team capacity radar chart
- Operational action items

### Strategy & Governance (Amber)

**Components**:
- Board meeting preparation
- Strategic planning
- Financial position
- Decision frameworks
- Strategic action items

### Timeline (Week Calendar)

**Components**:
- Day-by-day breakdown
- Critical meetings
- Client deliverables
- Partnership activities

## Visualizations

### Service Package Analysis

**Type**: Horizontal bar chart
**Data**: Market fit scores (0-100%)
**Packages**: Venture IQ, Intelligence Audit, Innovation Compass

### Team Capacity Radar

**Type**: Radar/spider chart
**Dimensions**: Corp BD, AI/Tech, Multilingual, Strategic, Financial, Agritech
**Datasets**: Current team vs. projected with new hires
