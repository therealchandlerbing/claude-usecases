# 360 Social Impact Studios Brand Standards

**For Design Director Skill Integration**

---

## Purpose

This document provides comprehensive brand standards for 360 Social Impact Studios. When the Design Director skill is used to create materials for 360, these standards take precedence and guide all visual design decisions.

## Integration with Design Director

When creating 360-branded materials:

1. **Read this document first** before applying design-director techniques
2. **Use 360 brand palette** instead of exemplar colors (Stripe, Linear, etc.)
3. **Follow 360 logo placement** rules
4. **Apply 360 typography hierarchy** as specified
5. **Consider audience-specific** 360 guidance
6. **Validate against 360 quality checklist**

The Design Director's elevation protocol still applies, but with 360 brand elements as the foundation.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors (Use Most Frequently)

**Navy Blue (Primary Brand Color)**
- HEX: `#1E4D6B`
- RGB: 30, 77, 107
- **Use for:** Headers, primary text, professional documents, formal communications
- **Design Director note:** This replaces exemplar primary colors (like Stripe's #0A2540)

**Bright Blue/Cyan (Energy & Accent)**
- HEX: `#00A7E1`
- RGB: 0, 167, 225
- **Use for:** Accents, data visualization, callouts, interactive elements
- **Design Director note:** Use for visual interest, similar to how you'd use accent colors in Linear

### Secondary/Accent Colors

**Lime Green (Growth & Sustainability)**
- HEX: `#7AC143`
- RGB: 122, 193, 67
- **Use for:** Growth metrics, positive indicators, sustainability themes, success states

**Magenta/Pink (Impact & Community)**
- HEX: `#E6007E`
- RGB: 230, 0, 126
- **Use for:** Impact highlights, community themes, energy, attention

**Red (Urgency & Key Metrics)**
- HEX: `#ED1C24`
- RGB: 237, 28, 36
- **Use for:** Urgency, key metrics, important callouts, error states

**Orange (Warmth & Collaboration)**
- HEX: `#FF8C00`
- RGB: 255, 140, 0
- **Use for:** Warmth, collaboration themes, partnership content, warnings

**Yellow/Gold (Innovation & Opportunity)**
- HEX: `#FFD100`

**Black (Body Text)**

**White (Backgrounds)**

**Light Gray (Subtle Backgrounds)**
- HEX: `#F5F5F5`
- RGB: 245, 245, 245

## Color Application Patterns

### Safe Combinations (Always Work)

- Navy Blue + White
- Bright Blue + White
- Any primary color + White background
- Navy Blue + Bright Blue + White

### Dynamic Combinations (Use for Visual Interest)

**Sustainability/Environmental:**
- Lime Green + Bright Blue + White

**Community/Energy:**
- Magenta + Orange + White

**Global/Cultural (especially Brazil):**
- Orange + Magenta + Yellow (warm, vibrant)

**Professional/Corporate:**
- Navy Blue + Bright Blue + White (conservative)

### Combinations to Avoid

- ❌ Red + Green together (accessibility issue, color-blind users)
- ❌ Too many colors in one section (limit to 2-3)
- ❌ Yellow text on white (fails contrast standards)
- ❌ Low contrast combinations

### Data Visualization Color Sequences

**Single Data Series:**
- Use Bright Blue (#00A7E1) as default

**2-3 Data Series:**
- Navy Blue (#1E4D6B)
- Bright Blue (#00A7E1)
- Lime Green (#7AC143)

**4-6 Data Series:**
- Navy Blue, Bright Blue, Lime Green, Magenta, Orange, Yellow
- Order by importance, not rainbow order

**Diverging Data (Positive/Negative):**
- Positive: Lime Green (#7AC143)
- Negative: Red (#ED1C24) or Orange (#FF8C00)
- Neutral: Light Gray (#F5F5F5)

|---------------|-----------|-----------|------|
| Reports/Documents | `LOGO_White_BG_1.png` | Top left | 2-2.5 inches |
| Presentations (title slide) | `LOGO_White_BG_1.png` | Top center | 3-4 inches |

### Logo Placement Rules

**Minimum Sizes:**
- Full horizontal logo: 2 inches / 150 pixels minimum width
- Circle badge: 0.75 inches / 75 pixels minimum width
- Never scale below these minimums

**Clear Space:**
- Maintain space equal to height of "360" on all sides of logo
- No other elements should intrude into clear space
- This ensures logo prominence and readability

**Logo Don'ts:**
- ❌ Never stretch or distort logo
- ❌ Never change logo colors
- ❌ Never place logo on busy backgrounds (use white or solid color box)
- ❌ Never use low-resolution versions (maintain at least 150 DPI)
- ❌ Never add effects (shadows, glows, outlines)

## Typography

### Font Stack

**Headings:**
1. Montserrat Bold (preferred)
2. Arial Bold (fallback)

**Subheadings:**
1. Montserrat SemiBold (preferred)
2. Arial Bold (fallback)

**Body Text:**
1. Open Sans Regular (preferred)
2. Arial Regular (fallback)

**Captions & Fine Print:**
1. Open Sans Regular (preferred)
2. Arial Regular (fallback)

### Typography Hierarchy

**Level 1 - Main Titles**
- Size: 28-36pt
- Weight: Bold (700)
- Color: Navy Blue (#1E4D6B) or Black (#231F20)
- Use for: Document titles, main headers, presentation titles

**Level 2 - Section Headers**
- Size: 18-22pt
- Weight: Bold (700) or SemiBold (600)
- Color: Navy Blue (#1E4D6B) or Bright Blue (#00A7E1)
- Use for: Major sections, slide titles

**Level 3 - Subsection Headers**
- Size: 14-16pt
- Weight: SemiBold (600)
- Color: Navy Blue (#1E4D6B) or Black (#231F20)
- Use for: Subsections, minor headers

**Body Text**
- Size: 10-12pt (12pt preferred for readability)
- Weight: Regular (400)
- Color: Black (#231F20)
- Line height: 1.5-1.6
- Use for: Paragraphs, descriptions, content

**Captions and Fine Print**
- Size: 8-9pt (minimum 8pt)
- Weight: Regular (400)
- Color: Mid-gray or 70% black opacity
- Use for: Image captions, footnotes, credits

### Design Director Integration

When applying Design Director typography techniques to 360 materials:

- ✅ Use Design Director's hierarchy principles (2.5-3x size relationships)
- ✅ Apply Design Director's line height recommendations (1.5-1.7x)
- ✅ Use Design Director's spacing and proximity rules
- ⚠️ Replace exemplar fonts with 360 font stack
- ⚠️ Use 360 colors for headers (Navy Blue primary, Bright Blue accents)

## Document Templates

### Standard Report Format

**Page Setup:**
- Margins: 1 inch all sides
- Page size: 8.5 x 11 inches (US Letter)
- Orientation: Portrait (Landscape for wide tables only)

**Header:**
- Logo: `LOGO_White_BG_1.png`, top left, 2 inches wide
- Document title: Aligned right or below logo
- Date and version: Small text below title
- Clear space around logo maintained

**Footer:**
- Page numbers: Bottom center or right
- Optional: Circle badge logo (`Circle__Logo_White_BG.png`) bottom left, 0.75 inch
- Contact info or confidentiality notice: Bottom center, 8pt

**Content Structure:**
1. Title page with full logo, document title, date, author
2. Executive summary or overview (if applicable)
3. Main content with clear section headers
4. Visual breaks using color bars or graphic elements (at 20-30% opacity)
5. Appendices and references

### Presentation Format (PowerPoint/Google Slides)

**Slide Dimensions:**
- Standard: 16:9 widescreen ⭐ **Preferred**
- Alternate: 4:3 standard (for specific venues only)

**Title Slide:**
- Logo: `LOGO_White_BG_1.png`, center or top center, 3-4 inches wide
- Presentation title: Large (32-36pt), bold, navy blue (#1E4D6B)
- Subtitle/date: Smaller (18-20pt), gray or black
- Presenter name and role
- Optional: Background graphic element (orbital symbol at 10-20% opacity)

**Content Slides:**
- Logo: `Circle__Logo_White_BG.png`, top right or footer, 1-1.5 inches wide
- Title: Top left or center, navy blue (#1E4D6B), 28-32pt
- Content: Bullets or paragraphs, 16-20pt minimum
- Accent color bar optional for visual interest (Bright Blue or accent colors)

**Section Divider Slides:**
- Full graphic mark background (`Graphic_LOGO_whiteBG_1.png`) at 20-30% opacity
- Section title large and centered
- Use accent colors (Bright Blue, Lime Green, Magenta) for variety

### One-Pager Format

**Layout:**
- Logo: `LOGO_White_BG_1.png`, top center, 2.5-3 inches wide
- Title: Bold, large (24-28pt), immediately below logo
- Content: Two or three columns
- Key stats or callouts: Use accent colors and larger text (16-18pt)
- Footer: Contact info, website, small circle badge

## Accessibility Standards

### Color Contrast (WCAG AA Compliance)

All text must meet 4.5:1 contrast ratio for normal text:

**Tested and Approved for normal text:**
- ✅ Navy blue (#1E4D6B) on white: **PASS** (9.0:1)
- ✅ Magenta (#E6007E) on white: **PASS** (4.5:1)
- ✅ Black (#231F20) on white: **PASS** (16.3:1)

**Use with caution:**
- ⚠️ Bright blue (#00A7E1) on white: **FAIL for normal text** (2.8:1). Only use for large text (18pt+ or 14pt bold+) or on dark backgrounds.
- ⚠️ Lime green (#7AC143) on white: **FAIL for normal text** (2.2:1). Only use for large text (18pt+ or 14pt bold+) or on dark backgrounds.

**Known Failures:**
- ❌ Yellow (#FFD100) on white: **FAIL** (1.46:1) — background accents only, never text

### Font Sizes

**Minimum sizes for accessibility:**
- Body text minimum: 10pt (12pt preferred)
- Caption text minimum: 8pt
- Presentation text minimum: 16pt (readable from distance)
- High-importance content: 14pt+

### Document Accessibility

- ✅ Provide alt text for all images with logos
- ✅ Use proper heading hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3)
- ✅ Ensure PDFs are screen-reader accessible
- ✅ Provide text descriptions for data visualizations
- ✅ Don't convey information by color alone (use icons, labels, patterns)
- ✅ Include focus states for interactive elements

## Quality Standards

### Hand-Crafted Appearance

Every 360 document must look intentionally designed, not template-based:

- ✅ Deliberate white space (not default padding)
- ✅ Typography relationships (not just bigger/bold)
- ✅ Color relationships (not generic defaults)
- ✅ Visual hierarchy (not just headings/body)
- ✅ Considered alignment (not just left-everything)

### Brand Consistency

- ✅ Logo present and correctly sized
- ✅ Colors match exact hex codes
- ✅ Fonts follow specified hierarchy
- ✅ Tone appropriate for audience
- ✅ Accessibility standards met

### Design Director Standards

- ✅ Would pass Design Director Phase 5 quality validation
- ✅ Portfolio-ready quality
- ✅ No red flags (defaults, poor hierarchy, cramped spacing)
- ✅ Consistent technique application throughout
- ✅ Clear design intentionality
