## Usage Workflows

### Workflow 1: Generate Personas from Existing Data

**Steps:**
1. Read `README.md` sections: "Prerequisites" and "Instructions"
2. Gather your interview transcripts or research data
3. Invoke the skill with your data
4. Review both outputs (strategic + platform-ready)
5. Check quality assessment against `references/vianeo-scoring-rubric.md`
6. Iterate if needed based on recommendations

**Files Needed:** README.md, vianeo-scoring-rubric.md

---

### Workflow 2: Plan Persona Validation Research

**Steps:**
1. Read `README.md` section: "Persona Types Supported"
2. Review `examples/innovator-persona-inferred-example.md` to see what happens without research
3. Study `references/interview-question-templates.md`
4. Determine target score using `references/vianeo-scoring-rubric.md`
5. Plan interview count and schedule
6. Conduct interviews using question templates
7. Invoke skill with interview data

**Files Needed:** README.md, interview-question-templates.md, vianeo-scoring-rubric.md, innovator-persona-inferred-example.md

---

### Workflow 3: Improve Existing Personas

**Steps:**
1. Compare your personas to `examples/partner-persona-example.md`
2. Use `references/vianeo-scoring-rubric.md` to identify your current score
3. Check "Improvement Pathways" section in rubric
4. Use `references/interview-question-templates.md` to design targeted validation
5. Collect additional data addressing specific gaps
6. Re-invoke skill with enhanced data

**Files Needed:** vianeo-scoring-rubric.md, interview-question-templates.md, partner-persona-example.md

---

### Workflow 4: Prepare for Vianeo Submission

**Steps:**
1. Review `README.md` section: "Quality Scoring Alignment"
2. Examine `templates/platform-ready-template.md` for format requirements
3. Generate personas using skill (target Score 4+ per rubric)
4. Review quality assessment
5. Address any flagged gaps
6. Use platform-ready output for Vianeo entry
7. Keep strategic version for scoring defense

**Files Needed:** README.md, platform-ready-template.md, vianeo-scoring-rubric.md

## Common Questions

### Q: Where do I start?
**A:** Read `README.md` first for comprehensive overview. Then review examples to see what outputs look like.

---

### Q: I have interview data. Now what?
**A:** Follow instructions in `README.md` "Step 2: Invoke the Skill" section. Provide your data and specify persona type if known.

---

### Q: How do I know if my personas are good enough?
**A:** Use `references/vianeo-scoring-rubric.md` to assess quality. The skill also provides automatic quality assessment in outputs.

---

### Q: I need to conduct interviews. What should I ask?
**A:** Use `references/interview-question-templates.md` to design interview guides aligned with the four-layer structure.

---

### Q: What's the difference between the two output templates?
**A:** Strategic version is comprehensive for internal use. Platform-ready version is condensed and constraint-compliant for Vianeo entry. See templates for details.

---

### Q: Can I see examples before using the skill?
**A:** Yes. Review both examples: `partner-persona-example.md` (Score 4, validated) and `innovator-persona-inferred-example.md` (Score 2, inferred).

---

### Q: What score do I need for Vianeo?
**A:** Score 3 is adequate. Score 4-5 is competitive. See `references/vianeo-scoring-rubric.md` for details.

---

### Q: How many interviews do I need?
**A:** For Score 4-5: 5-10+ per persona. For Score 3: 3-5. See rubric for details and `interview-question-templates.md` for research planning.

---

### Q: What if I don't have interviews yet?
**A:** Skill can generate inferred personas from available data to guide research planning. See `innovator-persona-inferred-example.md` for what this looks like.
