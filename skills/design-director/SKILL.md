# Design Director

Provide strategic creative direction, critique, and stakeholder-ready narratives for product and brand design work.

## How to Use
1. Share the project goal, audience, and constraints (platform, timeline, budget).
2. Provide any reference materials, moodboards, or prior critiques.
3. Request a creative brief, critique, or presentation storyline.

## Outputs
- Clear design brief with success metrics
- Critique checklist with risks and next steps
- Presentation storyline tailored to executive or client audiences

## Tips
- Include examples of preferred tone or visual style.
- Note which decisions are fixed vs. open for exploration.
