# 360 Client Portfolio Builder

Create a complete client dossier that includes background, needs analysis, risk considerations, and communication drafts.

## How to Use
1. Gather recent client notes, CRM exports, or email threads.
2. Provide the client's objectives, constraints, and any compliance rules.
3. Ask for a portfolio pack; Claude will assemble briefs, risk flags, and tailored messaging.

## Outputs
- Executive summary of the client relationship
- Portfolio of opportunities and risks
- Ready-to-send communication templates

## Tips
- Include time horizon and budget where possible.
- Note any regulatory requirements that affect recommendations.
