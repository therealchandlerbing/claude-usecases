# 990-EZ Preparation - Quick Start Guide

**Get your nonprofit's Form 990-EZ prepared in under 2 hours with guided automation.**

---

## 5-Minute Setup

### Step 1: Check Eligibility
Your organization qualifies for 990-EZ if:
- Gross receipts < $200,000
- Total assets < $500,000
- Not a donor advised fund sponsor or hospital operator

### Step 2: Gather Your Documents
Have ready:
- [ ] Financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet)
- [ ] EIN and legal organization name
- [ ] Board roster with titles and hours
- [ ] Program descriptions and metrics
- [ ] List of donors $5,000+ (for Schedule B)

### Step 3: Start Preparation
Say to Claude:
```
"Prepare our 990-EZ for 2024"
```

## Filing Package Contents

When complete, you receive:
1. **Form 990-EZ** - Complete, ready to file
2. **Schedule A** - Public Charity Status
3. **Schedule B** - Contributors $5,000+ (IRS only)
4. **Schedule O** - Supplemental Information
5. **Public Copy** - For disclosure (no Schedule B)
6. **Validation Report** - All checks documented
7. **Pre-Filing Checklist** - Final review items

---

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Gross receipts too high" | Must file full Form 990, not 990-EZ |
| "Balance sheet doesn't balance" | Check: Assets = Liabilities + Net Assets |
| "Program ratio below 65%" | Review expense allocation methodology |
| "Missing Schedule B" | Required if any donor gave $5,000+ |

## Important Deadlines

| Fiscal Year End | Filing Deadline | Extension To |
|-----------------|-----------------|--------------|
| December 31 | May 15 | November 15 |
| June 30 | November 15 | May 15 |
| Other | 5th month, 15th day | +6 months |

**Extension**: File Form 8868 before deadline for automatic 6-month extension.

## Next Steps After Generation

1. **Review** all numbers against source documents
2. **Get approval** from board (recommended)
3. **Obtain signatures** from President and Treasurer
4. **File electronically** via IRS e-file or authorized provider
5. **Complete state filings** if required
6. **Post public copy** to your website

---

## Quick Tips

- **Program descriptions**: Be specific with numbers (X people served, Y outcomes achieved)
- **Expense allocation**: Document your methodology for audit trail
- **Governance**: Having conflict of interest policy = "Yes" on Part V
- **Schedule B**: Keep confidential - NOT included in public copy
