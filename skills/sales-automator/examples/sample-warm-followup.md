# Sample Warm Follow-Up Campaign

**Scenario**: Following up with GlobalTech Solutions after 3-month gap in conversation

**Target**: Marcus Johnson, Director of Partnerships at GlobalTech Solutions

**Background**: Had initial discovery call in August. They were interested but timing wasn't right due to Q3 budget freeze.

---

## Intelligence Gathered

### Relationship History
- **Last Contact**: August 15 (3 months ago)
- **Previous Interaction**: 45-minute discovery call
- **Their Situation**: Budget frozen through Q3, asked to reconnect in Q4
- **Pain Points Discussed**: Manual partnership tracking, no centralized deal pipeline
- **Objections**: Wanted to see proof of ROI, concerned about implementation time

### Recent Developments
- **Web Search**: GlobalTech just announced Q4 earnings beat (30% revenue growth)
- **Calendar**: No meetings scheduled since August call
- **Gmail**: Last email from Marcus: "Let's revisit this in October when budgets reopen"
- **Asana**: Deal task exists, stage: Discovery, marked "Cold"

### New Positioning Angles
- Q4 budgets should now be available
- New case study since August: Partnership team scaled from 5 to 20 deals/month
- Reduced implementation time: Now 2 weeks vs. 4 weeks (addressing their objection)

---

## Email Sequence (3 Touches, 14 Days)

### Email 1: Reference Past + New Value
**Subject**: Following up on our August partnership discussion

**Send**: Day 0

```
Hi Marcus,

It's been 3 months since we talked about partnership tracking and pipeline management for GlobalTech.

You mentioned budget would reopen in Q4 and timing would be better to revisit. I also saw GlobalTech crushed Q4 earnings - congrats on the 30% growth!

I wanted to reach out because we've made some meaningful improvements since August that directly address the concerns you raised:

**What's new since we last talked**:
- Implementation time reduced from 4 weeks to 2 weeks (you mentioned speed was important)
- New case study: Partnership team at MediaCorp scaled from 5 to 20 active deals/month using our system
- ROI calculator that shows payback in 60 days based on deal velocity improvement

**Why it matters for GlobalTech**: With your growth trajectory, you mentioned your partnerships team is underwater managing deal flow manually. These improvements make it faster and lower-risk to test if our approach works for you.

Would it make sense to reconnect for 20 minutes to see if the timing's better now?

I'm available [Day 1] or [Day 2] next week.

Best,
Chandler

P.S. - How did that Q3 partnership with Acme Corp end up going? You mentioned it was a big one.
```

**A/B Test Subjects**:
- Option A: "Following up on our August partnership discussion"
- Option B: "Q4 timing for GlobalTech partnership tracking"
- Recommended: Option A (references past conversation specifically)

### Email 2: Specific Proposal
**Subject**: Partnership proposal for GlobalTech

**Send**: Day 5 (if positive response or no response to Email 1)

```

Based on our August conversation, I've put together a specific proposal for how we could help GlobalTech scale your partnerships operations.

**Scope**:
- Partnership pipeline tracking in Asana (30+ deals)
- Automated follow-up sequences for warm partners

**Timeline**: 2-week implementation + ongoing optimization
**Investment**: $50K annual subscription
**Expected Outcome**: 3x increase in active deals managed, 40% improvement in deal velocity

I've attached the full proposal with:
- Detailed approach based on your team's workflow
- MediaCorp case study (similar team size, 4x deal throughput)
- ROI calculation (60-day payback based on your numbers)
- Implementation plan with milestones

Would Tuesday or Thursday work for a 30-minute call to walk through it and answer questions?

Best,
Chandler

[Attach: GlobalTech_Partnership_Proposal.pdf]
```

## Optimization Notes

- **Why this works**:
  - Respects their timeline (they asked for Q4 follow-up)
  - References specific pain points from August call
  - Addresses their objections proactively (speed, ROI)
  - Includes new developments that strengthen case

- **Risk**: Marcus may have moved on to another solution during 3-month gap

- **Mitigation**: If no response by Day 10, LinkedIn research to confirm he's still at GlobalTech and in same role

- **Follow-up**: If proposal accepted, fast-track to contract (they've already been through discovery)

## Template for Other Warm Follow-Ups

Use this structure for any prospect you've talked to before:

1. **Research Current State**: What's changed since last contact?
2. **Reference Past Conversation**: Show you remember specifics
3. **Provide New Value**: What's different now that might change their decision?
4. **Make It Easy**: Low-friction next step
5. **Respect Their Time**: Acknowledge gap, give them permission to say no

**Key Differences from Cold**:
- Shorter sequences (3 vs. 5 touches)
- Faster follow-up cadence
- Reference specific past discussions
- Higher urgency (they're warm, not cold)
- Proposal can come earlier (Day 5 vs. Week 3)
